My Top 3 Favourite Movies

Princess Mononoke

A Miyazaki film that explores the relationship between extractivism and the environment with two protagonists fighting to save a forest.

Frances Ha

A film that explores the trials and tribilations of adulthood in in New York city as a dancer grapples with changing relationships and career instibility.

Interstellar

A film about a team of astronaughts that leave their families behind to look for a viable alternative planet for people on Earth to move to and the challenges they face along the way.
